Srijan's Recipie For the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough :

  • Warm water
  • teaspoon yet
  • cup sugar
  • egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling :

  • Brown sugar
  • ground cinnamon
  • butter

Instruction

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs
  2. Melt the butter add to mixture
  3. Add in the flour and mix into combine into a dough
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes
  5. Transfer the dough into large bowl
